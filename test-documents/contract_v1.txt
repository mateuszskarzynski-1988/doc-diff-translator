SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Acme Corp ("Client") and Vendor Ltd ("Provider") on January 1, 2024.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Provider shall deliver consulting services as outlined in Schedule A, attached hereto and incorporated by reference.

2. TERM
This Agreement commences on January 1, 2024 and continues for an initial term of twelve (12) months.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT
The Client agrees to pay the Provider a monthly fee of $5,000, payable within thirty (30) days of receipt of invoice. Late payments may incur a 1.5% monthly interest charge.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary information disclosed during the course of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years following termination.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice. Upon termination, the Client shall pay all outstanding fees for services rendered up to the termination date.

6. LIABILITY
The Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by the Client in the preceding six (6) months.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

8. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations.
